LuxStrands Knowledge Base

Find answers to common questions about our products, orders, and services.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also log into your LuxStrands account and view your order history for tracking information.

What is the best way to care for my LuxStrands hair?

To ensure the longevity of your LuxStrands hair, we recommend using sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners. Gently detangle with a wide-tooth comb from ends to roots. Air dry whenever possible, or use a low heat setting with heat protectant. Store your hair in a satin bag when not in use.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unused and unopened products within 14 days of purchase. Please ensure the original packaging and tags are intact. For hygiene reasons, opened or used hair products cannot be returned. Refer to our full Returns & Exchanges page for more details.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within Nigeria. International shipping times may vary. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. Please note that processing times (1-2 business days) are separate from shipping times.

Do you offer custom wig services?

Yes, LuxStrands offers custom wig creation services. You can choose your desired hair type, length, texture, and color. Please contact our customer support team or visit our 'Custom Orders' page for more information and to get a personalized quote.
